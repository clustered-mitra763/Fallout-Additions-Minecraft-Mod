## 🎮 Overview

Fallout-Additions-Minecraft-Mod transforms your standard Minecraft game into a survival experience inspired by the Fallout video game series. This modification adds various items, gear, and creatures to your world. You encounter wasteland environments, scavenge for supplies, and face threats like ghouls and super mutants. Use classic tools like the Pip-Boy to manage your health and navigate the terrain. This project focuses on balance and gameplay depth to offer a fresh take on block-based survival.

## ⚙️ Installation Steps

Follow these steps to prepare your game environment:

1.  Open your web browser and go to the official Forge website.
2.  Download the Forge installer that matches your Minecraft version, which is 1.12.2 for this mod.
3.  Run the installer. Select Install Client and click OK.
4.  Open the Minecraft Launcher.
5.  Select the Forge installation from the menu on the bottom left.
6.  Click Play to start the game and confirm that Forge loads correctly.
7.  Close the game.
8.  Press the Windows key and R key on your keyboard at the same time.
9.  Type %appdata% into the box and click OK.
10. Open the .minecraft folder.
11. Locate the mods folder. If you do not see one, create a new folder and name it mods.
12. Move your downloaded Fallout-Additions file into this mods folder.

## 🕹️ Starting the Game

Launch the Minecraft application from your computer Desktop. Select the Forge profile you installed earlier. When the main menu loads, you see a Mods button. Click this to verify the Fallout-Additions-Minecraft-Mod appears in the list. Create a new world to begin your adventure.

## 🗺️ Gameplay Features

The world generates new structures and loot based on the Fallout theme. Keep track of these features:

*   **Pip-Boy Interface:** Access your inventory and statistics through the custom Pip-Boy menu.
*   **Radiation Mechanics:** Monitor your radiation levels. Radioactive zones damage your health over time. Use Stimpaks to heal.
*   **New Mobs:** Explore deserts and ruins to find Ghouls and Super Mutants.
*   **Resource Gathering:** Scavenge ruins for Bottle Caps. Use these as currency for item trading.
*   **Power Armor:** Craft or find armor sets to increase your damage resistance and jump height.
*   **Nuka-Cola:** Find this beverage in chests to restore hunger and provide short stat boosts.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, check these common solutions:

*   **Game Crashes:** Ensure you have enough memory allocated to Minecraft. Open the launcher, edit your Forge profile, and increase the RAM allocation.
*   **Mod Not Visible:** Confirm the mod file sits directly inside the mods folder and not in a subfolder.
*   **Visual Glitches:** Update your graphics card drivers through your manufacturer software.
*   **Missing Textures:** Reinstall the mod file. If the problem persists, check for other conflicting mods in your folder.
